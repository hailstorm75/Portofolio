About Me

Hello! I'm Denis, a passionate developer who loves to create and isn't afraid to try new things. I've started my journey with LEGO® Mindstorms - a great toy for exploring your creativity. This led me into a rabbit hole of various tools and areas of the computing world and gave me a whole new perspective on how everything works.

Skipping a few years ahead, I land my first developer contract for a language-teaching company to develop complementary interactive media content for their textbooks. From that moment, it was clear that this career path was an obvious choice for me.

Today I am a developer with 5+ years under my belt and a passion for writing reliable, readable, and tested applications without compromising performance or documentation. I am fortunate to work for both a small and a corporate companies. There I've gained experience in areas such as working in a team, leading a project, starting new projects, and evolving old ones.

Here's what I've explored so far in the .NET space:

  • WPF
  • xUnit
  • Avalonia
  • Specflow
  • WinForms
  • D365 SDK
